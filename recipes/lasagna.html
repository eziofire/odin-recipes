Caramel Macchiato

Description

Caramel Macchiato is espresso-based beverage sold in Starbucks.

Ingredients

  • steamed milk
  • espresso
  • vanilla syrup
  • caramel drizzle

Steps

  1. Milk and vanilla syrup must be put into the cup first so that they're at the bottom of the cup and then the espresso so that it sits on top.
  2. The caramel drizzle is added last, sitting on top of the drink, not stirred in
