Apple pie

Description

An apple pie is a pie in which the principal filling ingredient is apples.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Flour
  • sugar
  • milk
  • cinnamon
  • butter
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat apples
  2. Cover and Rest
  3. Dry apples
  4. Add sugar, Cinnamon, Salt, and Cornstarch
  5. Toss to Combine
  6. Line and Fill Pie Plate
  7. Cover With Top Crust
